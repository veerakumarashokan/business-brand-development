# Packaging

This folder contains all product packaging and label designs.

## Includes

- Soap labels  
- Herbal powder packaging  
- Hair oil labels  
- Signature bar packaging  

## Purpose

To standardize product presentation and ensure consistency across all packaging.

## Notes

- Follow fixed dimensions (e.g., 6x4 cm, 8x10 cm)  
- Maintain alignment with brand style  
- Ensure print-ready quality (high resolution)

# Designs

This folder contains all visual design work related to the Saara Herbals brand and products.

## Structure

- banners – Marketing banners and promotional visuals  
- logo – Brand logos and variations  
- packaging – Product label and packaging designs  

## Purpose

To develop and maintain a consistent visual identity across all products and marketing materials.

## Includes

- Soap label designs  
- Herbal powder packaging  
- Hair oil label design  
- Poster and banner creatives  

## Notes

- Follow consistent typography and color system  
- Maintain alignment with brand identity  
- Keep final and working versions organized separately if needed
